<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/sum-of-ap-series4512/1">Sum of AP series</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Basic</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">A series with same common difference is known as arithmetic series. The first term of series is '<strong>a</strong>' and common difference is <strong>d</strong>. The series looks like a, a + d, a + 2d, a + 3d, . . . Find the sum of series upto <strong>nth</strong> term.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n<strong> = </strong>5, a = 1, d = 3
<strong>Output: </strong>35
<strong>Explanation: </strong>Series upto 5th term is 1 4 7 10 13, so sum will be 35.
</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n<strong> = </strong>3, a = 1, d = 2
<strong>Output: </strong>9
<strong>Explanation: </strong>Series upto 3rd term is 1 3 5, so sum will be 9.
</span></pre>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Constraints:</strong><br>1 ≤ n ≤ 500<br>0 ≤ a, d ≤ 500</span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Mathematical</code>&nbsp;<code>Algorithms</code>&nbsp;
