<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/count-number-of-words1500/1">Count Words in a String</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Easy</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">Given a string <strong>s</strong> consisting of lowercase English alphabets, spaces, tab characters (\t), and newline characters (\n), <strong>count</strong> the total number of <strong>words</strong> present in the string. A word is defined as a continuous sequence of lowercase English letters, while spaces, tabs, and newline characters act as separators between words.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input:</strong> s = "abc def"
<strong>Output:</strong> 2
<strong>Explanation:</strong> There is a space at 4th position which works as a seperator between "abc" and "def".
</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input:</strong> s = "a\nyo\t"
<strong>Output:</strong> 2
<strong>Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">There are two words in the string: "a" and "yo". The characters \n and \t act as separators, splitting the string into words.</span></pre>
